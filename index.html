ABOUT US

At Sidecup Family Golf, we are dedicated to providing a fun and enjoyable experience for the whole family. Our state-of-the-art Toptracer Range allows you to practice your golf skills while enjoying a meal or drink from our cafe. We also offer golf lessons for all skill levels and an adventure golf course for those looking for a bit of fun.

TOPTRACER RANGE

Experience the latest in golf technology with our Toptracer Range. Track your shots, improve your game, and have fun with friends and family.

GOLF LESSONS

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced golfer, our professional instructors are here to help you improve your game with personalized lessons.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Enjoy a fun-filled day with our adventure golf course. Perfect for families and friends looking for a unique and exciting experience.
